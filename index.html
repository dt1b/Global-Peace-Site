Aditya

Child of God

I am a child of God. I have all the divine properties of my father Shiv Baba.

Divine Virtues

  • Love
  • Peace
  • Purity
  • Happiness
  • Bliss
  • Knowledge
  • Joy

I am filled with following divine Virtues

  1. I am a loveful soul.
  2. I am a peaceful soul.
  3. I am a pure soul.
  4. I am a happy soul.
  5. I am a blissful soul.
  6. I am a joyful soul.
  7. I am a Knowledgeful soul.
